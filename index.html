We Are Wirlix




Wirlix is a social media debate platform sparking controversy and increasing the interactivity between individuals to institute change. We are giving millennials a voice in regards to world-wide issues today. Our vision is to empower the younger generation to not only engage in powerful discussions but also come together to creatively make an impact and resolve today's biggest controversies on college campuses and beyond.
